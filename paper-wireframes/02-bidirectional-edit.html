I need a training intake form for new fitness coaching clients.

Collect basic client information: name, age, weight, height, and email.

Assess fitness baseline: current activity level, training frequency per week, preferred session duration (30/45/60/90 min), and any prior training experience.

Record health and injury history: general injury notes structured injury records including body region, injury type, date of injury, and current status (active/recovered/managed), allergies, and current medications.

Capture training goals: primary goal (hypertrophy / strength / endurance / weight loss / rehabilitation), target muscle groups if applicable, and timeline expectations.

Include a movement screening section: FMS scores for deep squat, hurdle step, inline lunge, shoulder mobility, active straight leg raise, trunk stability push-up, and rotary stability.
